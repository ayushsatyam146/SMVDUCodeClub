About Us



We are a group of Computer Enthusiasts who focus on something more preliminary and relevant, ”A Love for Coding”. Every year Code Club plans to hold events for familiarizing students with UNIX tools, Linux environment, Git Development Workflow, practice Algorithms and Data Structures,Machine Learning ,besides conducting workshops on GUI programming, Web development, Android application Development and Hackathons.

We organize activities related to Development (Web Development, Open Source, Game/App Development), programming contests, hackathons, Capture The Flag contests, et all. Lecture series are regularly taken on algorithms, web hijacking, development, among other things.

Code Club is a collective efforts of students and alumni of Shri Mata Vaishno Devi University,Katra. Our aim is to increase the coding culture at our university and make coding a essential part of technical life.It provides coders a platform to showcase their skills and compete with their fellow coders.
