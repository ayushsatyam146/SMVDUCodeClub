GOOGLE EXPLORE ML!

Note from the author:

Explore Machine Learning (ML) is a Google-sponsored program for university students to get started with Machine Learning. The curriculum offers 3 tracks of Machine Learning Content (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced) and relies on university student facilitators to train other students on campus. In order to help equip student facilitators to train others, Google hosts a series of Machine Learning Academies. This course is aimed to make students self-sufficient to pursue ML on their own. Explore ML is a 2-day training experience for student facilitators to get familiar with the programs’ content, meet one another and connect to Googlers.

We will be walking you through Explore ML, it’s modules and basic ML concepts. Additionally, we will have you go through selected modules of the course, with a teaching assistant guiding you in the course’s practical pieces/exercises and troubleshooting and pieces of the content where you are struggling.
